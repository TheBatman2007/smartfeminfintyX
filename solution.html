Our Mission

Creating Lasting Impact

SmartFem Cup addresses critical challenges in women's health and environmental sustainability

Environmental Crisis

One woman uses over 10,000 disposable pads in her lifetime. This creates massive environmental impact through plastic waste, clogged water systems, and contaminated soil. SmartFem Cup eliminates this waste entirely.

Health Revolution

Poor menstrual hygiene leads to infections and health complications. Our AI-powered tracking helps detect irregularities early, potentially preventing serious health issues through continuous monitoring and pattern analysis.

Social Equality

Period poverty affects millions globally. Our accessibility features, voice interface, and community programs break down barriers, reaching women in underserved regions and creating lasting social impact.
